WHAT SPRITES IS
Sprites.ai is an end-to-end AI marketing platform. Unlike tools like ChatGPT or Gemini which give insights and recommendations but require manual execution, Sprites handles the full cycle in one place.
It connects directly to Meta and Google ad accounts, launches campaigns, shifts budget toward what is converting, creates content, and publishes SEO blogs directly to Shopify or CMS. All automated with human review and approval before anything goes live.
CORE DIFFERENTIATOR
Most teams use AI tools like ChatGPT or Gemini to get insights then manually execute everything themselves. Sprites closes that execution gap entirely.
ChatGPT can write a blog. It cannot publish it, embed schema, connect to an ad account, or shift budget. Sprites does all of it automatically.
Think of Sprites as the Claude-powered ad bot that Jayant Joshi's setup guide is pointing toward - except it is already built, already integrated, and works out of the box. No API connections to configure, no prompt engineering, no debugging when your ad account does something unexpected. You describe what you want and it executes.
WHAT SPRITES DOES
Connects natively to Meta Ads and Google Ads accounts via API
Pulls historical campaign data and surfaces insights automatically
Builds and launches campaigns autonomously
Shifts budget toward converting campaigns in real time
Creates ad content including static images and copy
Researches keywords and writes SEO blog posts
Embeds JSON-LD schema markup for AI search engine visibility (GEO)
Publishes content directly to Shopify or CMS
Provides unified dashboard across multiple brand accounts
Creates individual client or brand profiles so AI understands each brand separately
Monitors performance and flags underperforming campaigns automatically
SEO audit - surfaces missing headings, broken links, alt text gaps, page speed, sitemap issues
AI SEARCH ENGINE VISIBILITY (GEO)
Sprites analyzes each client's current AI visibility score, implements JSON-LD structured data schema across their pages so AI engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity can read and cite their content, and publishes SEO optimized blog content automatically.
When a potential customer asks an AI search engine a question the client should answer, the client's brand shows up. This is a standalone pitch angle most prospects have not considered.
