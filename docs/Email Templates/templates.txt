OBJECTION HANDLING:

OBJECTION: We already use ChatGPT
RESPONSE: ChatGPT gives you recommendations but you still have to manually execute everything. Sprites connects directly to your Meta and Google accounts and actually launches the campaign, moves the budget, and publishes the content. That last mile is where we are different.

OBJECTION: We have an agency
RESPONSE: A lot of our customers actually use Sprites alongside their agency to get better visibility into what the agency is actually doing. It surfaces exactly what is performing and what is not so you are not relying on their report to know what is happening.

OBJECTION: Is this just a GPT wrapper
RESPONSE: A GPT wrapper puts a different interface on ChatGPT. Sprites has direct API integrations into Meta and Google that let the AI actually execute inside your ad accounts. ChatGPT cannot move your budget or launch a campaign. Sprites can.

OBJECTION: We do not have budget right now
RESPONSE: We have a $60 per month proof of concept tier specifically for this. Connect two or three brand accounts, run it for 30 days, and build the case for the bigger budget conversation.
