# ICP Signals — Who Converts and Why
## Extracted from Sprites.ai pipeline (March 2026)

## Prospect Profiles That Converted or Are Converting

### Tier 1: Highest Conversion Probability
**Multi-brand ecom operators** (27 August Apparel pattern)
- $100K+ monthly ad spend across multiple brands
- Paying $100K-$200K/yr to agency and unhappy with ROAS
- Shopify Plus or similar enterprise ecom platform
- Founder/operator directly manages ad accounts
- Signals: asks about attribution, mentions "revenue leakage", wants to bring ops in-house
- Close rate: Fastest — audit-first approach, 4-6 week pilot

**Marketing agencies scaling client portfolios** (Chris Moulton pattern)
- 10-50 contractors/employees
- Serving Series A+ startups or mid-market
- Running Meta, Google, LinkedIn for clients
- Signals: asks about multi-client management, white-label, per-brand pricing
- Close rate: Medium — want to evaluate on their own terms, give materials

### Tier 2: Strong Fit, Needs Nurturing
**Solo marketing consultants adding services** (Hassan Ali pattern)
- Creative specialist wanting to add media buying
- Small team (1-5 subcontractors)
- B2B niche focus
- Signals: "I don't do media buying but want to", "force multiplier", asks about learning curve
- Close rate: Medium — $500/mo entry, needs hand-holding during onboarding

**PE-backed or multi-brand portfolio operators** (Kevin Gilsdorf pattern)
- Managing 5-10 brands for portfolio company
- Diverse client types (nonprofit, sports, startups)
- Budget-conscious, wants to scale gradually
- Signals: asks about tiered pricing, "can I start with one brand?", has internal champion
- Close rate: Medium — trial approach, 2-week validation cycle

### Tier 3: Warm But Slow
**Solo in-house marketers at constrained companies** (Olivia Gomez pattern)
- Only marketer at company
- Limited budget, no authority to buy
- Signals: "I need to justify this to my boss", "not the right time", "limited budget"
- Close rate: Low/slow — keep warm, follow up monthly, arm with ROI data

## Conversion Signals (from actual calls)

### Strong Buy Signals
- Asks about onboarding timeline ("when can we start?")
- Discusses future expansion ("what about Amazon/TikTok?")
- Brings up specific integration needs ("does it work with Klaviyo?")
- Has direct access to ad accounts and credentials
- A second person validates ("my assistant thinks this looks solid")
- Asks about billing details and credit usage
- References competitor tools they've tried and didn't like

### Neutral Signals
- Asks lots of feature questions without connecting to their pain
- "This is interesting" without specific next steps
- Requests case studies or documentation to review later

### Red Flags
- "I need to talk to my boss" without a timeline
- "Not the right time" without suggesting when
- Asks about free tier only
- No questions about implementation or onboarding
- Solo marketer with no budget authority

## ICP Scoring Framework
- **Ad spend >$50K/mo**: +3 points
- **Multiple brands/accounts**: +2 points
- **Currently paying agency >$5K/mo**: +2 points
- **Direct credential access**: +2 points
- **Asks about audit or attribution**: +2 points
- **Has internal champion/validator**: +1 point
- **B2B niche with defined audience**: +1 point
- **Solo marketer with no budget**: -2 points
- **"Not the right time" with no timeline**: -1 point

**Score 8+**: Hot — push for pilot
**Score 5-7**: Warm — trial with follow-up
**Score <5**: Nurture — monthly touchpoints
