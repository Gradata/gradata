## Common Objection Patterns

### Pricing/Budget
- "We don't have budget for new tools" → Free trial, low entry point, ROI proof first
- "The agency costs less" → Show 10-20x cost reduction vs agency, agency overhead math

### Timing/Readiness
- "Not the right time" → Low-pressure follow-up, keep door open, schedule next touch
- "Need to talk to my boss" → Arm them with ROI data and case study to present

### Capability Doubt
- "I'm not technical enough" → AI handles complexity, you just approve
- "Can it really beat my agency?" → Specific ROAS numbers from comparable clients

### Commitment Fear
- "What if it doesn't work?" → 4-6 week pilot, one brand only, no long-term contract
- "I need to see results first" → Free trial with credits, audit before commitment
