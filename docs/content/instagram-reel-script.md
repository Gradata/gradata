## Production notes

- Very little text on screen at a time. IG audience skews to atmosphere over info density.
- Keep voiceover to one line at 0:08. Let the visuals carry the rest.
- All emotional beats, zero metrics. The TikTok and X thread carry the numbers.
- Shot list: keyboard close-up, brain animation (After Effects), two chat window mocks (Day 1 / Day 7), logo reveal.
- Color grade: cool blues in the frustration beats, warm amber in the payoff.
