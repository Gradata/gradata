# Discovery Question Library for Sprites

## Opening Questions (Warm Up)

These set the tone. Goal: get them talking about their world, not answering your checklist.

- "Walk me through how your team handles paid media today — who touches what?"
- "What's your biggest frustration with how campaigns get managed right now?"
- "If you could wave a magic wand and change one thing about your marketing ops, what would it be?"

## Gap Questions (Current vs. Desired State)

These reveal the distance between where they are and where they want to be.

- "What would it mean for your team if campaign optimization was automated?"
- "Where are you spending time that you know isn't the highest-value use of your day?"
- "How much visibility do you actually have across all your brand accounts right now?"
- "If I pulled up your ad account right now, would you be able to tell me what's working and what's wasting money?"

## Implication Questions (Surface Hidden Costs)

These make the pain tangible and quantifiable. When they say the number out loud, they sell themselves.

- "When your agency takes 3 days to make a campaign change, what happens to performance in that window?"
- "How much are you spending per brand per channel on retainers right now?"
- "If your organic traffic is flat, what's the long-term cost of ignoring SEO?"
- "How many hours per week does your team spend on manual reporting?"
- "What's the cost of running campaigns without real-time budget optimization?"

## Confirmation Questions (Validate Understanding)

These build trust and prevent misalignment.

- "So if I'm hearing you right, the core issue is [X]. Is that fair?"
- "It sounds like the biggest frustration is [X]. What am I missing?"
- "Just to make sure I'm not off base — your top priority right now is [X], correct?"

## Sprites-Specific Discovery Tracks

### Multi-Brand Operator
1. "How many brands are you managing ads for right now?"
2. "Do you have a unified view across all of them, or is each one siloed?"
3. "What does it cost you to not have that single pane of glass?"

### Solo Consultant / Agency
1. "How many client accounts are you managing?"
2. "How much time do you spend inside ad platforms vs. doing strategy?"
3. "What would it mean if the platform work was automated and you could focus on client relationships?"

### Lean Marketing Team
1. "Is there one person handling all of paid, organic, and content?"
2. "What falls through the cracks when that person is busy with something else?"
3. "Have you looked at AI tools to close that gap, or has it been mostly manual so far?"

### Company with Agency Pain
1. "How's your current agency relationship going?"
2. "Do you feel like you have full visibility into what they're doing with your spend?"
3. "If you could see every campaign, every dollar, every result in real-time — would that change how you make decisions?"
