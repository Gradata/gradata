# Cadence Templates for Sprites.ai

## 12-Touch, 3-Week Cadence

### Email 1: The Cold Intro (Day 1)
```
Subject: [2-4 words, lowercase, relevant to their world]

Hi [First Name],

[One sentence observation about them or their company — specific, not generic]

[One sentence naming the problem they likely have]

[One sentence proof — case study with a specific number]

[One sentence CTA — simple yes/no question or meeting link]

Best,
Oliver Le
Account Executive, Sprites.ai
```

### Email 2: The Case Study (Day 3)
Lead with the story, different angle from email 1.
```
Subject: re: [original subject] OR [new short subject]

Hi [First Name],

[Company name] was in a similar spot — [one sentence describing their situation].

[One sentence what changed]. [One sentence result with specific metric].

Want to see how this would work for [their company]?

Best,
Oliver Le
Account Executive, Sprites.ai
```

### Email 3: The Value-Add (Day 7)
Give something useful. No hard ask.
```
Subject: [topic relevant to their pain]

Hi [First Name],

[Share a stat, insight, or trend relevant to their role].

[One sentence connecting it to a problem they face].

Thought this might be relevant. Happy to walk through what we're seeing if useful.

Best,
Oliver Le
Account Executive, Sprites.ai
```

## Angle Rotation for Sprites Sequences

- **Email 1:** Execution gap (AI insights vs. AI execution)
- **Email 2:** Case study (H.M. Cole ROAS or Iris Finance SEO)
- **Email 3:** GEO / AI search visibility angle or industry stat
- **Email 4:** Social proof stack (multiple case studies)
- **Email 5:** Breakup — soft close, leave the door open

## Reply Handling

### Positive ("Interested, tell me more")
Don't dump info. Move to a call.
"Great to hear. I'd love to show you what this looks like for [Company] specifically. Would [day] or [day] work? [Book a call here](https://calendly.com/oliver-spritesai/30min)"

### Neutral ("What exactly do you do?")
Two sentences max, then pivot.
"Sprites connects to your Meta and Google accounts and automates campaign management, budget optimization, and SEO content. The best way to see it is a quick walkthrough — [Book a call here](https://calendly.com/oliver-spritesai/30min)"

### Negative ("Not interested")
Be gracious. Don't argue.
"Appreciate you letting me know. If things change down the road, I'm here. Best of luck with [something specific about their company]."

### Objection reply ("We use [competitor/agency]")
"Makes sense. The difference our clients find is that Sprites actually executes inside the ad accounts, not just recommends. H.M. Cole replaced their agencies entirely. Happy to show you the comparison if useful."
